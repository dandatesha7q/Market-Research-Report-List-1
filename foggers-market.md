<p><ul><li>Thermal Foggers</li><li>Cold Foggers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foggers are devices used for dispersing pesticides, sanitizers, or insect repellents, categorized into thermal and cold foggers. Thermal foggers heat the solution to create a fine mist, ensuring deeper penetration into vegetation and effective pest control. In contrast, cold foggers use air pressure to atomize the solution without heating, making them ideal for sensitive environments, such as indoor spaces or food storage areas. Both markets cater to agricultural, residential, and industrial applications, offering distinct advantages based on user needs.</p></p>
