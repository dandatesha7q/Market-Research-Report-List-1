<p><strong>The Bolted Bonnet Gate Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Socket Weld Connections</li><li>Flanged Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bolted bonnet gate valve market comprises two primary connection types: socket weld connections and flanged connections. Socket weld connections involve welding a pipe directly to the valve, ensuring a secure and leak-proof fit, commonly used in high-pressure applications. Flanged connections, on the other hand, utilize flanges bolted together, allowing for easier assembly and disassembly, making them suitable for maintenance and retrofitting. Both types are essential in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
