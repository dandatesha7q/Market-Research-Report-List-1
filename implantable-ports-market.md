<p><p>The implantable ports market is segmented into two main types: single-lumen and double-lumen implantable ports. Single-lumen implantable ports are designed for a single route of access to deliver medications or fluids, making them suitable for patients requiring less complex treatments. In contrast, double-lumen implantable ports offer dual access, allowing simultaneous administration of multiple medications or infusions without interference, which is beneficial for patients with more demanding treatment regimens. Both types enhance patient comfort and provide easier long-term vascular access.</p></p>
