<p><strong>The Spray Guns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Type</li><li>Pressurised Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spray guns market is primarily categorized into two types: ordinary and pressurized. Ordinary spray guns operate using a simple mechanism where paint is drawn from a container by gravity or siphon, making them suitable for smaller projects. In contrast, pressurized spray guns utilize an external air compressor to atomize paint, allowing for a finer spray and greater control, making them ideal for larger applications or professional use. Each type caters to different needs based on project scale and finish quality.</p></p>
