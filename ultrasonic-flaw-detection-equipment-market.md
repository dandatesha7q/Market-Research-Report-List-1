<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment</li><li>Phased Array Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment</li><li>TOFD Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic flaw detection equipment is crucial in non-destructive testing to identify internal flaws in materials. Conventional ultrasonic flaw detection uses a single transducer to transmit and receive sound waves, detecting defects with a straightforward approach. Phased array ultrasonic flaw detection utilizes multiple transducers to steer and focus sound beams, enhancing detection sensitivity and precision. Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD) equipment measures the time it takes for sound waves to reflect off defects, providing accurate depth information and detailed analysis of flaws.</p></p>
