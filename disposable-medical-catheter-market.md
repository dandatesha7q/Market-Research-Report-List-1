<p><p>The disposable medical catheter market comprises various types, including intermittent catheters, Foley catheters, and urological catheters. Intermittent catheters are used for short-term bladder drainage and promote self-catheterization. Foley catheters, designed for continuous drainage, are used in hospital settings for patients requiring long-term catheterization. Urological catheters encompass a broader category, including both intermittent and Foley types, tailored for various urological conditions. The market is driven by increasing incidence of urinary disorders and a growing preference for disposable options to reduce infection risks.</p></p>
