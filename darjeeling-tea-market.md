<p><strong>The Darjeeling Tea Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whole Tea Leaves</li><li>Broken Leaves</li><li>Fannings</li><li>Dust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Darjeeling tea market consists of four main types based on the size and quality of the leaves. Whole tea leaves are prized for their delicate flavor and are often used in premium blends. Broken leaves, while slightly lower in quality, still provide a robust taste and are commonly used in loose-leaf teas. Fannings are smaller broken leaves that infuse quickly, making them popular in tea bags. Dust consists of the smallest particles, often used in instant teas, providing strong flavor but lacking the nuanced qualities of larger leaves.</p></p>
