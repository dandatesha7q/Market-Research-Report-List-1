<p><p>The oncology/cancer drugs market includes various drug types aimed at treating cancer. Antimetabolites disrupt cellular metabolism, interfering with DNA synthesis. Alkaloid drugs, derived from plant sources, inhibit cell division. Hormonal therapies target hormone-sensitive cancers by blocking hormones' effects. Targeted drugs focus on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, improving precision in treatment. Platinum-based drugs, such as cisplatin, damage DNA to kill cancer cells. The "Other" category encompasses emerging therapies, immunotherapies, and novel agents not classified elsewhere.</p></p>
