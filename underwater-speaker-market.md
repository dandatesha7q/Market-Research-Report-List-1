<p><p>The underwater speaker market comprises three main types: portable speakers, flush mount speakers, and floating-on-top speakers. Portable speakers are designed for easy mobility and can be used in various aquatic settings. Flush mount speakers are permanently installed in walls or surfaces, delivering a seamless look and sound experience. Floating-on-top speakers are designed to rest on the water's surface, providing sound without submersion. Each type caters to different user preferences and environments, enhancing aquatic audio experiences.</p></p>
