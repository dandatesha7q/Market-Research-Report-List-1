<p><ul><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The line pipe market comprises various types, primarily LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes. LSAW pipes are characterized by their longitudinal welding method, offering high strength and large diameters, ideal for high-pressure applications. SSAW pipes utilize a spiral welding technique, providing flexibility and efficiency for transportation and structural applications. Other market segments include seamless pipes, which offer superior pressure tolerances, and ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes, known for lower production costs and rapid turnaround.</p></p>
