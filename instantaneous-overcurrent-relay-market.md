Electromagnetic Relays rely on the movement of an electromagnetic coil to detect overcurrents rapidly. Amperometric Relays utilize current measurement methods to identify excess current and initiate protective actions. Other market types include digital relays, microprocessor-based devices, and innovations that combine features of both previous types, enhancing reliability and responsiveness in electrical protection systems across various applications and industries.</p></p>
