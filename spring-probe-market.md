<p><p>The Spring Probe Market can be divided into two main types: Standard Type and Double Plunger Type. Standard Type spring probes are widely used for general-purpose applications, offering reliable electrical connections with moderate contact force. In contrast, Double Plunger Type probes provide enhanced performance by utilizing two plunger mechanisms, allowing for improved electrical contact and stability, making them ideal for high-precision applications. Each type serves distinct needs within various industries, such as electronics manufacturing and testing.</p></p>
