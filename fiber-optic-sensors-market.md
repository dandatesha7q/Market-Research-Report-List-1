<p><strong>The Fiber-Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber-optic sensors are categorized into two main types: intrinsic and extrinsic. Intrinsic fiber-optic sensors operate based on changes in the light properties within the fiber itself, making them sensitive to temperature, pressure, and strain. In contrast, extrinsic fiber-optic sensors use external optical components, such as mirrors or beamsplitters, to detect environmental changes, allowing for greater versatility in monitoring parameters like displacement and vibration. Both types serve various applications in industries such as oil and gas, civil engineering, and healthcare.</p></p>
