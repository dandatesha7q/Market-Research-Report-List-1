<p><strong>The Acrylic Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic</li><li>Cast Acrylic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acrylic products market primarily categorizes into extruded acrylic and cast acrylic. Extruded acrylic is produced by forcing the material through a mold, resulting in uniform thickness and lower cost, making it suitable for applications like signage and displays. In contrast, cast acrylic is made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, leading to superior optical clarity, custom shapes, and higher durability, which makes it ideal for items like windows, aquariums, and high-end retail displays.</p></p>
