<p><p>The Electric Discharge Machine (EDM) market primarily includes two types: Electric Spark Forming Machines and EDM Wire Cutting Machines. Electric Spark Forming Machines utilize controlled sparks to shape conductive materials, ideal for intricate designs and precision components. In contrast, EDM Wire Cutting Machines employ a thin wire electrode to cut through metal, ensuring high accuracy in complex geometries. Both types are essential in manufacturing sectors, enhancing production efficiency and enabling the creation of detailed parts with minimal material wastage.</p></p>
