<p><p>The garden tractor market is primarily divided into two main types: wheeled tractors and crawler tractors. Wheeled tractors are popular for their maneuverability on flat terrains, making them suitable for mowing, tilling, and other landscaping tasks in residential gardens. Crawler tractors, equipped with tracks instead of wheels, are designed for rugged terrains and provide better stability and traction on uneven ground. Both types cater to different gardening needs, with wheeled tractors favored for smaller spaces and crawler tractors for tougher conditions.</p></p>
