<p><p>The on-site hydrogen generator market primarily consists of two types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers and PEM Electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers utilize a liquid alkaline solution to facilitate hydrogen production through electrolysis, offering cost-effectiveness and established technology. In contrast, PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers employ a solid polymer membrane, enabling rapid response and higher purity hydrogen production, making them ideal for dynamic applications. Both technologies cater to various industries, including chemical manufacturing, energy storage, and fuel cell applications.</p></p>
