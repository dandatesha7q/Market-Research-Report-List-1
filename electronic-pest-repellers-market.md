<p><p>The electronic pest repellers market is primarily divided into two types: electromagnetic and ultrasonic devices. Electromagnetic repellers emit low-frequency electromagnetic waves that target pests' nervous systems, effectively disrupting their communication and behaviors. In contrast, ultrasonic repellers produce high-frequency sound waves that are inaudible to humans but create a distressing environment for rodents and insects, driving them away. Both types aim to provide eco-friendly solutions for pest control without the use of chemicals, appealing to environmentally conscious consumers.</p></p>
