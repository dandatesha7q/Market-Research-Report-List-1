<p><p>The sweeper truck market features three main types: Mechanical Broom Sweepers, Regenerative-Air Sweepers, and Vacuum Sweepers. Mechanical Broom Sweepers utilize rotating brushes to gather debris, ideal for heavy dirt on various surfaces. Regenerative-Air Sweepers use a combination of air suction and a blowing system for efficient dust and debris removal, suitable for environmentally sensitive areas. Vacuum Sweepers employ powerful suction to collect fine particles and litter, making them ideal for urban environments with high cleanliness standards.</p></p>
