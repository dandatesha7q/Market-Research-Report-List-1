<p><p>The Location-Based Time and Attendance System market includes various technologies for tracking employee attendance. Proximity cards allow users to clock in/out by swiping cards near readers. Biometrics use unique physical traits like fingerprints or facial recognition for identification. Time cards involve traditional manual entry on paper systems. Web-based login stations enable remote access through computers or mobile devices. Interactive Voice Response (IVR) allows employees to log their hours via phone systems, providing flexibility and ease of use for attendance management.</p></p>
