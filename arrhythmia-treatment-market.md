<p><p>The arrhythmia treatment market encompasses various approaches, including medication, cardioversion, catheter ablation, pacemakers, and implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs). Medication involves drugs to manage heart rhythm. Cardioversion uses electrical shocks to restore normal rhythm. Catheter ablation targets and destroys abnormal heart tissue causing arrhythmias. Pacemakers are implanted devices that regulate heartbeats, while ICDs monitor and correct life-threatening arrhythmias through shocks. Together, these treatments cater to diverse patient needs, enhancing cardiac health and quality of life.</p></p>
