<p><p>Post-applied waterproofing membranes are essential in protecting structures from water infiltration. Sheet-based membranes are pre-manufactured rolls that are adhered to surfaces, offering quick installation and consistent thickness. These membranes are typically made from materials like bitumen, PVC, or TPO. In contrast, liquid-applied membranes are applied as a liquid and cure to form a seamless, flexible barrier. They can easily conform to intricate shapes and joints, providing excellent coverage and reducing the risk of leaks in complex applications.</p></p>
